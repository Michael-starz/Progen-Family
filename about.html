Building and maintaining love through sacrifices

Family is not merely determined by genes, instead it is built and maintained by love and sacrifices

This organization has been established to not only to focus on providing adoption services to prospective adoptive parents or adoptee (child), but to also have a refined method as regards the way these people are supported in the adoption process.

Adoption is a sensitive yet overwhelming process that exert excessive mental stress and psychological trauma on the applicant as well as the adoptee, hence we are looking on creating a positive experience for both parties involved. We believe that we can the right guidance and support needed to become an adoptive parent, where people are more important than the process.

We have systematically improved the method of recruiting, preparing and assessing prospective parents for adoption. We are committed to delivering comprehensive post-adoption services aimed at supporting our adopters through every stress encountered living as an adoptive family.

Our Approach

We work based on the standard procedures of adoption within the state; hence we will provide services ranging from :

  1. Preparation stage
  2. Assessment stage
  3. Matching stage
  4. Follow-up stage

The establishment through the trained professional in our team will provide high standard support in all stages of the process through counselling, support groups, casework interventions and advocacy through a hybrid process
